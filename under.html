UNDERGROUND CROP HEALTH DETECTOR

Underground crop health detector helps to improve yield

About Us

This is a modern farming technique that is used to know the health of the underground crop like carrot, radish, beetroot, potato etc..

While the crop is being planted, we place them in a certain order such that all the sapling as in a particular row or column We will make a map of where the saplings are the distance between each row and other information and feed it into the system.

What this machine does is that when we turn it on it starts moving according to the map without overlapping the saplings and it digs a hole within 20cm radius beside the sapling and measures the temperature of the soil.

For a crop to be productive we much check the temperature, if the temperature is good the yield is good. Also, it scans the ground and detect the length and size of the vegetable. It helps us to know the progress of the crop how much it is growing day by day.

BENEFITS OF UNDERGROUND CROP HEALTH DETECTOR

1)This technique boosts up the confidence of the farmer as he can know the daily progress.
2) Helps to know the progress of the crop
3) To know the size and length of the crop
4)Helps farmers to know the underground temperature
