About Us

Vence raises $12m to enable rotational grazing with virtual electric fences

Grazing your animals has never been easier. With Vence, you can control the movement and grazing of your animals directly from your smartphone or using a schedule. Vence uses artificial intelligence to optimize rotational or strip grazing to maximize the yield of your land - so you can rest easy knowing that your livestock are getting the best care possible.

Keep your animals healthy and safe with Vence. Our app monitors the well-being of your animals, collecting data from sensors in our lightweight devices worn by livestock. Whether you're checking in on your cattle during a thunderstorm or tracking their grazing patterns, Vence makes it easy to stay informed about the health of your herd. Stay one step ahead with Vence!

Virtual fencing solutions allow customers to increase revenues, reduce expenses and unlock their lands to sustainable grazing land management practices without the burden of increased labor. Users can control the movement and grazing of their animals directly from a smartphone or using a built-in schedule, maximizing the yield of their land and minimizing costs. The virtual fences can be placed anywhere on a property using the Vence smartphone or web app, and customers can employ static or dynamic boundaries to dictate grazing behavior or specify protected areas and waterways.
